The above figure depicts the connections between the characters of major houses in the TV series Game of Thrones. The figure was made from the directed graph data of Game of Thrones characters. The cells contain the number of edges between the two houses considering the direction. The number of edges were normalized according to the following formula:

\[ S_{st} = \frac{{N_{st} \times N}}{{N_s \times N_t}} \]

Where \( N_{st} \) is the number of edges between source node's house \( s \) and target node's house \( t \), \( N \) is \( \sum_{st} N_{st} \), \( N_s \) is \( \sum_t N_{st} \), and \( N_t \) is \( \sum_s N_{st} \). If the value is larger than 1 the connection is frequent. Most houses have more connections among themselves than with other houses. The most cliquey houses are Martell and Greyjoy. The Tully and Arryn are close to each other, which is story accurate since they are allies in the series.
