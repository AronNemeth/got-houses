The figure portrays the connections between characters of major houses from the TV series Game of Thrones only including houses that are part of the two alliances who fight in the war. It was generated from the directed graph of the characters. The cells contain the number of edges normalized according to the following formula:

\[ S_{st} = \frac{{N_{st} \times N}}{{N_s \times N_t}} \]

Where \( N_{st} \) is the number of edges between source node's house \( s \) and target node's house \( t \), \( N \) = \( \sum_{st} N_{st} \), \( N_s \) = \( \sum_t N_{st} \), and \( N_t \) = \( \sum_s N_{st} \). The threshold value is 1, so a value greater than 1 shows that the connection is more prevalent, than what we would expect in case of independence, if it is smaller than 1 it is less prevalent. The figure is in line with the story of the series. The houses show cohesiveness, since the values on the diagonal are above threshold. Apart from this the two major alliances are also depicted on the figure. The Stark, Tully and the Arryn houses form one community (rebels) and the Lannisters, Baratheons and Tyrells form another (royalists).
